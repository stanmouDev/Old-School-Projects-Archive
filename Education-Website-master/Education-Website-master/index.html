The Advantages of an OpenLabs Education

Formerly known as NIIT Ghana, OpenLabs are technology centers across West Africa aimed at improving career outcomes and digitally upskilling the youth of Africa, having trained over 30,000 students in Ghana, Liberia & Sierra Leone since 1999.

Our most popular programs are the 24 months Diploma courses, immersive technology curriculums designed to bring out the engineer in you, and prepare you for the most demanding jobs in tech. They are recognized by corporates across banking, finance, technology, startups and enterprise technology.
